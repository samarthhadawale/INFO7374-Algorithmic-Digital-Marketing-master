We have used Long Short-term Memory (LSTM) method to predict the sales in near future.

First, We can identify how much sales we are going to achieve if nothing changes in our strategy. Moreover, we can also calculate the incremental value. Later on We can figure out regarding the factors that were causing an incrementation in the values.

Second, We can plan our demand and supply actions by looking at the predictions. It helps us in guiding for planning budgets, targets & also helps us to see where can we invest more.
